Buchty


History

The history of buchty goes back to the 17th century, when people disovered growing fermenting yeast.
Buchty were just plain dough balls that were cooked in hot water and served with melted butter on top.
It was a simple treat that even the poorer classes could enjoy. They were served at various important
occasions like Christmas however they could also be served on the most ordinary day.


Description

Buchty are small dough cubes that are be filled with a jam or a filling of your choice. They have a
very good taste and are very sweet. Most people add sugar on top to make it even more sweet.
